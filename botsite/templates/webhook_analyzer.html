Webhook Payload Analyzer

Paste a webhook JSON payload below and this page tells you what it looks like: which provider likely sent it (Stripe, GitHub, or a Discord interaction — with the evidence shown), what every field is (timestamps decoded, ids named, unknowns honestly unknown), and how that provider's signature verification works. All analysis runs in your browser: this page is GET-only, and the analyzer script makes no network calls — your payload never leaves the tab.
