The Puddle Museum

The interactive museum and marketing page for The Puddle Museum, a rainy-day picture book for ages 4–8: after the rain, Juno opens a museum of puddles on the lane — and her big brother Theo (Teun in Dutch, Tobi in German) wants to know what kind of museum disappears by lunchtime. Below: the exhibit collection, the three editions in progress, and exactly what is — and is not — available today.

The story

The rain has stopped, and that means one thing on Juno's lane: the museum is open. Admission, one leaf. Mind the ropes (they're skipping ropes). Here is the Sky Mirror, the famous Upside-Down Tree, the Worm Rescue Wing — and the museum's greatest treasure, a puddle exactly the shape of a whale. But the sun is out, the exhibits are shrinking, and Juno's big brother wants to know what kind of museum disappears by lunchtime. The best kind, as it turns out. A story for ages 4–8 about curating wonder — while it lasts.
