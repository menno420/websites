The meta-point

None of these failures were hidden, and most produced a durable artifact: a regression test, a ledger entry with the exact error text, a doctrine section, a guard script. That loop — fail, record, harden, and let the next session inherit the hardening instead of the failure — is the actual mechanism behind the growth numbers, more than any single session being clever.
