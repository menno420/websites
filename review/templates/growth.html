Why it could move this fast

  • Merge is the deploy — no release train; a green required check is the entire distance between an idea and production.
  • Parallel lanes with a conflict-free bus — one writer per file means ten lanes never block each other on coordination.
  • Unattended chains — sessions schedule their own successors; the 26-slice overnight run is most of day three's bar.
  • Discovered cost is paid once — every wall goes into the capability ledger with its exact error and workaround, so no later session re-pays it.
  • The gates scale with the speed — the test count chart is the counterweight to the PR chart; both grew together, on purpose.
