Discord sign-in is not configured

This deployment has no Discord OAuth application — no client id, no secret, no redirect URI. That is deliberate, not an outage.

{{ icon("shield", 16) }}

Why there is no sign-in here

The armed control panel — where Discord sign-in identifies WHO is making a change so the bot can re-check their permissions on every write — is designed to live in a separate service from this read-only dashboard, and only after the owner decides where live bot control lives (open decision Q-0004). Until then this service reads no OAuth configuration at all: there is nothing to misconfigure and nothing to leak.

What the future armed service needs (an owner action: the OAuth app, its redirect URI, and the env wiring) is written down in docs/specs/bot-control-api-v1.md and docs/owner/OWNER-ACTIONS.md — the env-var names deliberately live in those docs, not in this code.

Meanwhile every dry-run action here is honestly attributed to anonymous (OAuth not configured).
